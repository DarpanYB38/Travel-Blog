Travel Costs from India

Accommodation Cost: Budget: $10 - $30 per night
Mid-range: $30 - $100 per night
Luxury: $100+ per night

Daily Expenses:Budget: $20 - $40 per day
Mid-range: $40 - $100 per day
Luxury: $100+ per day (food, transport, etc.)

Currency

Currency: Indian Rupee (INR)
Approx. 1 USD = ₹82

Climate:India has a diverse climate, ranging from tropical monsoon in the south to temperate in the north. The climate varies from hot and humid in the coastal regions to cooler temperatures in the Himalayas.

Beautiful Places to Visit

  • Taj Mahal, Agra:One of the Seven Wonders of the World, a symbol of love and Mughal architecture.
  • Jaipur, Rajasthan:Known as the Pink City, famous for palaces and forts like Amber Fort and City Palace.
  • Kerala: Known as "God's Own Country," famous for its backwaters, beaches, and lush greenery.
  • Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh:One of the oldest living cities in the world, known for its ghats along the Ganges River.
  • Goa:Famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.
  • Leh-LadakhKnown for its stunning landscapes, monasteries, and adventure tourism.

Popular Visitor experiences

  • Golden Triangle Tour:Covering Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, this tour provides a comprehensive experience of India's rich history and architecture.
  • Houseboat Stay in Kerala:Experience the tranquil backwaters on a traditional houseboat.
  • Desert Safari in Rajasthan:Explore the Thar Desert with camel rides and luxury camping.
  • Spiritual Retreats: Places like Rishikesh, Varanasi, and Dharamshala offer spiritual retreats, yoga, and meditation.
