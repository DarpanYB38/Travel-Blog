Australia

A vast, diverse country known for its stunning natural landscapes, unique wildlife, and vibrant culture, offering a unique experience for visitors from India.

Travel Costs from India

Flight Cost: Approx. ₹50,000 - ₹1,00,000 (Round Trip)

Accommodation Cost: Approx. ₹5,000 - ₹15,000 per night

Daily Expenses: Approx. ₹3,000 - ₹7,000 per day (food, transport, etc.)

Currency

Currency: Australian Dollar (AUD)
Approx. 1 AUD = ₹53

Climate:Mostly arid or semi-arid, temperate in the southeast and southwest, tropical in the north.

Beautiful Places to Visit

  • Sydney:Famous for the Sydney Opera House and Sydney Harbour Bridge
  • Great Barrier Reef:World’s largest coral reef system
  • Melbourne:Known for its arts, culture, and coffee scene
  • Uluru(Ayers Rock):Massive sandstone monolith in the heart of the Northern Territory
  • Great Ocean Road:Coastal road known for its stunning ocean views and the Twelve Apostles rock formations
