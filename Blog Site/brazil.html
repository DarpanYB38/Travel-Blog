About Brazil

A vibrant country with a diverse landscape, rich culture, and world-famous attractions, offering a unique experience for visitors from India.

Travel Costs from India

Flight Cost: Approx. ₹80,000 - ₹1,50,000 (Round Trip)

Accommodation Cost: Approx. ₹5,000 - ₹15,000 per night

Daily Expenses: Approx. ₹2,500 - ₹6,000 per day (food, transport, etc.)

Currency

Currency: Brazilian Real (BRL)
Approx. 1 BRL = ₹17.5

Climate: Brazil has a diverse climate, ranging from tropical in the north to temperate in the south. The Amazon basin is notably hot and humid, while the southern region experiences cooler winters.

Beautiful Places to Visit

  • Rio de Janeiro:Known for the Christ the Redeemer statue and stunning beaches.
  • Amazon Rainforest:The largest rainforest in the world, rich in biodiversity.
  • Iguaçu Falls:One of the largest and most impressive waterfall systems in the world.
  • Salvador:Famous for its Afro-Brazilian culture, historic sites, and vibrant festivals.
  • Pantanal:The world's largest tropical wetland, known for its wildlife.
