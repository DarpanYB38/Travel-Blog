About United Kingdom

A historically rich country known for its diverse landscapes, iconic landmarks, and vibrant culture, offering a unique experience for visitors from India.

Travel Costs from India

Flight Cost: Approx. ₹50,000 - ₹90,000 (Round Trip)

Accommodation Cost: Approx. ₹6,000 - ₹18,000 per night

Daily Expenses: Approx. ₹3,000 - ₹8,000 per day (food, transport, etc.)

Currency

Currency: British Pound Sterling (GBP)
Approx. 1 GBP = ₹104

Climate:Generally temperate, with plenty of rainfall throughout the year.

Beautiful Places to Visit

  • London:Buckingham Palace, Tower of London, Big Ben
  • Edinburgh:The historic and cultural capital of Scotland
  • Stonehenge:Prehistoric monument in Wiltshire
  • Lake District:National park known for its glacial ribbon lakes and rugged mountains
  • Bath:Famous for its Roman-built baths
