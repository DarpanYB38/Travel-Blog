About Russia

A diverse country with a strong economy, known for its technology, iconic landmarks, and varied climates, offering a range of experiences for visitors from India.

Travel Costs from India

Flight Cost: Approx. ₹40,000 - ₹80,000 (Round Trip)

Accommodation Cost: Approx. ₹3,000 - ₹12,000 per night

Daily Expenses: Approx. ₹2,500 - ₹6,000 per day (food, transport, etc.)

Currency

Currency: Russian Ruble (RUB)
Approx. 1 RUB = ₹0.85

Climate:Largely continental, with cold winters and warm summers. Siberia experiences subarctic and tundra climates.

Beautiful Places to Visit

  • Moscow:Red Square, Kremlin, St. Basil's Cathedral
  • St.Petersburg:Hermitage Museum, Peterhof Palace
  • Lake Baikal:World's deepest freshwater lake
  • Sochi:Famous for its beaches and as a winter sports destination
  • Trans-Siberian Railway:A journey across the vast landscapes of Russia
